Crime Rates and Population in Jacksonville, Florida

By Rachel Porter

Crime Rates Across Florida

The following chart compares violent crime rates across cities in Florida. The data was taken in 2017 as reported by police officers. The data reports that Jacksonville, Florida has the highest violent crime rate compared to the other cities. According to Neighborhood Scout's website, Jacksonville has one of the highest violent crime rates in the nation. They also found that Jacksonville is only safer than 9% of US cities. In Jacksonville, your chance of being involved in a violent crime is 1 in 158 people, comparatively, your chance of being involved in a violent crime in the state of Florida is 1 in 232 people.

Information from NeighborhoodScout.com

Population Size in Jacksonville

As seen from data above the violent crime rate in Jacksonville is the highest among cities in Florida. However, the data above also shows that Jacksonville has one of the highest populations in Florida. Of the cities listed above, Jacksonville had the largest population.
